IT Policies and Procedures

APS’s approach to IT Policies and Procedures establishes the rules of conduct within an organization, outlining the responsibilities of both employees and employers and also ensures that our client’s information is kept safe. Having these policies and procedures enables organizations to have the power to protect any information that is disclosed to them by unauthorized access and purposes by unauthorized individuals.

IT Strategic Planning

APS’s approach to IT Strategic Planning enables organizations to make decisions and implement solutions that support the overall strategic plan. We provide our clients with useful tools that guide day-to-day decisions, that also evaluate progress and changing approaches when moving forward.

ICT Governance

APS’s approach to ICT Governance ensures that the senior management of an organization retains control of , and responsibility for, its IT operation. We tailor make ways and methods through which an organization can implement, manage and monitor ICT governance. We also provide guidelines and measures to effectively utilize IT resources and processes within an organization.
